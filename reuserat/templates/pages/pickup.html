What Items Do You Accept?

Items qualify if they can fit into a 18 in x 24 in box.
However, there are exceptions for some large items that we also accept:
TVs, Mini Fridges, Microwaves, and Computer Monitors are all eligible for pickups.

Allowed Items

  • TVs
  • Speakers
  • Laptops & Computers
  • Lamps & Mini Fridges
  • Books & Movies
  • Jewelry & Watches
  • Crafts & Glass Items
  • Video Game Systems & Games
  • Kids Items
  • Appliances (Microwaves, Coffee Makers, Etc.)
  • Dorm Items (Lights, Small Tables, Etc.)
  • Items smaller than 18 in x 24 in
  • See Full List

Not Allowed Items

  • Mattresses
  • Clothing
  • Shoes
  • Items in poor condition
  • Items exceeding 18 in x 24 in (if not specified above)
