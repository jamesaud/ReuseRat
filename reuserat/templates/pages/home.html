Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get paid?

That depends! Within a few weeks you can expect a few of your items to sell. We post an item for a maximum of 70 days before moving it to clearance.

How do I start making money?
Sign Up Now For Free!
How much will my items sell for?

It's in our best interest to sell your items at a reasonable price. Using our proprietary algorithm, we lower the price of the item over time to increase it’s chances of selling.

What if an item doesn't sell?

If an item doesn't sell after 90 days, you have two options. You can choose for us to donate the item on your behalf, or we can send the item back to you.

Will I get reimbursed for shipping?

You sure will! Simply upload your shipping receipt and you'll receive store credit. Desire more? Follow us on social media for discounts!

Why don't you take clothing?

It’s difficult to do a quality check on used clothing. The good people at ThredUp focus on this task and does it better than us
