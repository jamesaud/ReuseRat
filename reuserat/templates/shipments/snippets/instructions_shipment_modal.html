Shipping Instructions: Quick Guide

1. Pack Your Box

  1. - Find or buy a quality shipping box that won't break (old amazon boxes work fine).
  2. - Pack your items in the box. Be sure to protect delicate items with bubble wrap!

2. Print Your Shipping Label

  1. - Print off your shipping label from the shipment detail page.
  2. - Using a standard printer is fine.

3. Attach Your Shipping Label & Tape the Box

  1. - Use strong tape (duck tape recommended) to tape the label to the side of the box.
  2. - Ensure all 4 sides are taped down, so there's no chance the label can fall off.
  3. - Tape the box generously with strong tape, ensuring it will not break during transit.

4. Pay for Shipping (You'll Be Reimbursed) & Send Your Box

  1. - Find you local USPS store.
  2. - Bring your box & to the drop off desk.
  3. - Request USPS Media Mail Shipping if all your items are books, CD's, or DVD's.
  4. - Request USPS Retail Ground Shipping if your items don't fit media mail criteria.
  5. - Important: upload your receipt on the shipment details page.
