Learn How to Ship in 2 Minutes

1. Choose Your Shipping Type - Pick the Option that Fits Best

  1. USPS Media Mail - Choose Media Mail if every item in your shipment is a Book, DVD, CD, or Magazine. (learn more)
  2. USPS Retail Ground - Choose Retail Ground if your items do not fit the media mail criteria. It typically takes 3-9 days for retail ground to arrive at our wearhouses. (learn more)

2. Print Your Shipping Label

Note: This is an unpaid shipping label. You will have to pay for the label at your local USPS office or online. You'll be reimbursed for shipping costs when the box arrives at our warehouse.
  1. Click the title of your shipment to see its details. Find the Shipping Label section.
  2. Print off the shipping label on standard 8 x 11 printer paper.

3. Attach Your Shipping Label

  1. Tape the shipping label to your package. Make sure to tape all four sides so that the table won't fall off.
  2. If you don't have strong tape, your local USPS post office can attach the label when you drop the box off.

4. Pay for Shipping & Send Your Shipment - Pick an Option

Now that you've printed off your shipping label, you'll have to pay for the shipping. Don't worry - you'll be reimbursed when the box arrives at our warehouse.
  1. Post Office (recommended) - Stop by your local post office, and pay for the option you chose in step 1. It is important that you include the receipt in the box, or upload a picture of the receipt to your shipment details page, so that we can reimburse you for shipping costs. (find post office)

  2. Online (more flexible) - Use an online provider like Stamps.com (free trial) or USPS.com (always free) for buying shipping labels. You'll needed to include our address on the new label, which can be found on your original shipping label. If you choose this option, the shipping label we provided in step 3 should be placed INSIDE the box. Tape your new shipping label to the package as specified in step 3.

    This option is more flexible. You may now drop off your box at a local USPS drop box, schedule a pick up on USPS.com, or stop by your local post office. Be sure to upload a picture of the shipping label to your shipment details page so we can reimburse you.
