Control How You Get Paid

1. Open Payment Settings

  1. From the dashboard, go to My Account.
  2. Click on the Payments tab.

2. Choose Your Payment Method

  1. Paypal (recommended) - Select the 'Paypal'' option and choose the email associated with your Paypal account. When you cash out an email will be sent requesting to transfer funds to your Paypal account. Don't see your email or want to use a different one? Go to the email tab to verify your email or add a new one.
  2. Direct Deposit - Choose the 'Direct Deposit' option and fill out your banking info. Make sure you have your checking number and account number handy. When you cash out money will be transfered to this bank account, usually within 3-5 business days.
  3. Check - Choose the 'Check' option. When you cash out, we'll send a check in the mail to your address on file. Remember, there is a $1 mailing charge on checks!

3. Cash Out

  1. When you have a balance greater than $5 you are able to cash out.
  2. From the dashboard, click the green button that says "Cash Out" next to your current balance.
  3. Confirm the amount and your payment method, and that's it! You should receive an email if your using Paypal, a check in the mail if using check, or a deposit in your bank account in 3-5 days in you are set up for direct deposit.
