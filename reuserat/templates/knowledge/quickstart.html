Start Selling Items & Making Money Quickly

1. Make Your Account

  1. First you need to register to start the process of sending your items. You can sign up here, it'll only take a minute!
  2. Fill out your profile information and choose a payment method. You can be paid via Paypal, Check, or Direct Deposit.
  3. Tip: If you ever get lost, just hit the dashboard button at the top.

2. Create Your First Shipment

  1. Click the create new shipment button on the dashboard.
  2. Next fill out your shipment information. Write a title to help you remember what's in this shipment. Then list the items going into this shipment in detail and describe their conditions.
  3. Remember, a shipment is a single box! If you need to send multiple boxes, please create multiple shipments.

3. Get Ready For Shipping

  1. Continue to the shipping guide and make your first shipment.
